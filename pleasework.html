It's Walnut Wednesday!


Today we are making a Walnut Carrot Cake!
Click me to see the ingredient list!


Lets begin:
Step 1: Preheat oven to 180C.
Step 2: Grease and flour a round cake tin.
Step 3: Sift together flour, baking powder, bicarbonate, cinnamon, salt.
Step 4: In a separate bowl combine together butter, brown sugar, caster sugar. Stir in eggs and orange juice. Add sifted dry ingredients and mix well. Finally add the walnuts and carrots.
Step 5: Pour the mixture into the prepared tin.
Step 6: Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven.
Step 7: Remove the cake and let it cool in the tin for 10 minutes. Then invert it onto a wire rack for futher cooling.
Step 8: To make icing, beat all icing ingredients together until smooth.
Step 9: Spread the icing over the cake once its' cool.

Final step: enjoy!
